Introducing OneSpark

We designed OneSpark because your content should adapt to you—not the other way around.

When brain-computer interface technology meets Artificial Intelligence to bring the world’s first smart headphones that personalize content to your brain: OneSpark.

Designed with brain-sensing AI that nudges you at the optimal time so you can focus back, OneSpark adapts to your learning needs in real-time. OneSpark also suggests new content based on your interests and past activity, keeping you inspired with relevant content.

Thanks to OneSpark’s sensor technology, you can monitor your attention span trends and your content consumption insights on the MyBrain App, helping you track your progress and achievements.

FAQ

1. What is Neocore Onespark Brainput?

A pair of EEG‑sensing headphones that reads your brainwaves from the ear canal and tweaks audio & text content in real time so it always “feels right.”

2. Why should I care?

No more zoning out, rewinding, or rereading. Neocore keeps you engaged, saves time, and boosts retention by matching content to your live attention state.

3. How does it actually work?

Tiny EEG sensors track your attention levels; an on‑device AI instantly adjusts pacing, language complexity, or emphasis so the material stays compelling.

4. What kinds of content can it adapt?

Podcasts, audiobooks, web articles, PDFs—basically any audio or text you choose to pipe through Neocore.

5. Will it change the author’s meaning?

No. The system alters style and pacing, not core ideas. You decide how much freedom it has—subtle tweaks or bolder edits.

6. Do I stay in control?

Yes. Toggle intervention level, get chime alerts before each tweak, or keep everything seamless—your call.

7. Who benefits the most?

Anyone battling distraction—students, busy professionals, commuters, people with ADHD—or anyone who just wants smarter content flow.

9. How can headphones in my ears read brainwaves?

Brain signals spread across your entire head—including the ear canal. Mini EEG sensors in each earpiece pick up those patterns—no bulky headband needed.

10. Is it really rewriting content on the fly?

Yes—think of it as “smart remixing.” The AI trims, rephrases, or re‑orders segments so the story stays intact but the delivery matches your focus and mood.

11. What signals tell Neocore I’m zoning out?

Our models monitor attention‑related EEG markers (e.g., shifting alpha, theta, and beta patterns). When engagement dips, the AI steps in.

12. Will I notice when it tweaks the content?

Only if you want to. Keep it invisible or get a quick chime right before each adjustment.

13. Does it learn my habits over time?

Absolutely. Neocore builds a private, on‑device profile of your attention patterns, so its tweaks get smarter every session.

14. What do other users prefer—seamless or notified tweaks?

Early testers are split: some love full invisibility; others enjoy knowing when a tweak lands. Try both and see what fits you.

15. What’s up with the name “Onespark Brainput”?

“Neocore” = your new cognitive core. “Onespark” nods to that single spark of insight. “Brainput” highlights direct brain input.
