Introducing OneSpark

We designed OneSpark because your content should adapt to you—not the other way around.

When brain-computer interface technology meets Artificial Intelligence to bring the world’s first smart headphones that personalize content to your brain: OneSpark.

Designed with brain-sensing AI that nudges you at the optimal time so you can focus back, OneSpark adapts to your learning needs in real-time. OneSpark also suggests new content based on your interests and past activity, keeping you inspired with relevant content.

Thanks to OneSpark’s sensor technology, you can monitor your attention span trends and your content consumption insights on the MyBrain App, helping you track your progress and achievements.

Benefits: Focus better – Learn smarter

  • Focus better
    • No need to push yourself to focus anymore
    • Stop feeling frustrated when losing your focus
  • Learn smarter
    • Stay on track with your learning content effortlessly
    • Understand when you focus best and least during your sessions

Features:

  • Focus shifts detection: OneSpark knows exactly when you get distracted and subtly nudges you to focus back.
  • Adaptive content delivery: OneSpark knows what type of information you need when you need it. It summarizes when your attention dips, simplifies when you are confused, or expands with more information when you are engaged.
  • Real-time attention tracking: OneSpark listens to your brain by using EEG technology and sensors to track your attention level.
  • Content consumption insights: OneSpark helps you understand your behaviours and be aware of your learning habits.
